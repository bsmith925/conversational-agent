# Welcome to RAG Assistant! 👋

Hi there! I'm your AI assistant powered by Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). I can help you find information from your knowledge base.

## How I Work

1. **Understanding**: I analyze your question and understand what you're looking for
2. **Retrieval**: I search through the knowledge base for relevant documents
3. **Generation**: I synthesize the information into a clear, accurate answer

## Tips for Better Results

- Ask specific questions
- Reference previous parts of our conversation - I remember context!
- If you need more detail, just ask for clarification

## Features

- 💬 Conversational context across multiple turns
- 📚 Source citations for all information
- 🔍 Transparent retrieval process
- ⚡ Real-time streaming responses
